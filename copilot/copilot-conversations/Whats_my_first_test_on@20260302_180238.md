**ai**: OPQRST is a patient assessment tool used in emergency medical services to gather information about a patient's chief complaint. It stands for:

- **O**nset: When did the symptom or illness begin?
- **P**rovocation and palliation: What makes it better or worse?
- **Q**uality: How does the symptom feel (e.g., sharp, dull, throbbing)?
- **R**adiation: Does the pain or symptom spread to other areas?
- **S**everity: On a scale of 1–10, how severe is it?
- **T**iming: How long has the symptom lasted?

This structured approach helps EMS providers quickly identify key details about a patient's condition and prioritize care. It is commonly used in conjunction with other assessment tools like SAMPLE and DCAP-BTLS to ensure comprehensive evaluation of the patient’s health status.
