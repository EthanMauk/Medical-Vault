The **Scene Size-Up** is the initial assessment performed upon arrival to the scene. Its primary goal is to protect yourself, your partner, and your patient. **You cannot provide care if you become a patient.**
### Scene Size-up Steps
- BSI/PPE
	- Use standard precautions and reassess BSI/PPE constantly
- Identify Environmental Hazards
	- Fire, Traffic, Instability, Weapons, Toxicity
- Personal, Partner and Patient safety
- Number of Patients
	- If there are multiple patients that you cannot care for at once activate [[Incident Command System|ICS]] and begin [[Triage|triage]] 
- Mechanism of Injury/Nature of Illness
- Additional Resources
	- ICS, ALS unit, Police/Fire Department, Hazmat
- Need for Specialized Equipment
	- Bariatric Equipment, Jaws of Life,
