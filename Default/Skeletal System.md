The **Skeletal System** provides structural support, attachments for soft tissues and internal organs, and produces blood cells. The Skeletal System is composed of **206** bones, which are commonly divided into the [[Axial Skeleton]] and [[Appendicular Skeleton]]. The pelvis includes bones from the Axial and Appendicular skeleton. 

In addition to bones, the Skeletal System is composed of:
- [[Joints]] 
- [[Ligaments]]
- [[Tendons]]
- Cartilage
