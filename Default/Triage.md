**Triage** is the process of sorting patients based on severity of injury or illness to prioritize treatment and transport when resources are limited.

## Triage Systems
### START (Simple Triage and Rapid Treatment)
Able to walk -> <font color="#00b050">Minor</font>
1. Respirations
	- Not breathing -> Open Airway -> <font color="#c00000">Immediate</font>
	- Respiratory Rate >30 -> <font color="#c00000">Immediate</font> 
2. Perfusion 
	- Capillary Refill > 2 Seconds ->  <font color="#c00000">Immediate</font> 
	- No Radial Pulse -> <font color="#c00000">Immediate</font> 
3. Mental Status
	- Cannot follow commands -> <font color="#c00000">Immediate</font> 
	- Follows Commands -> <font color="#fac08f">Delayed</font>
