**Good Samaritan Laws/Act** protect a layperson to take reasonable action when another persons life is at risk, and not to be liable for errors that are made in good faith emergency care. These only apply when there is no [[Duty to Act]].

For Good Samaritan to apply you must
- Have acted in good faith
- Rendered care without expectation of compensation
- Acted within the scope of your training
- Did not act in a [[Gross Negligence|grossly negligent]] manner
