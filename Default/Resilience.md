**Resilience** is an individuals ability to recover from [[Distress|distress]]. Resilience is a core aspect of [[Wellness]]. 

The following practices can help improve resilience:
- Eat a well balances diet
- Ensure 7-9 hours of sleep per day
- Strengthen positive relationships
- Build relationships with peers and colleagues
- Incorporate daily stretching, movement and exercise
- Practice mindfulness
