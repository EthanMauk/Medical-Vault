**Emergency Response Guidebook (ERG)** is a reference manual used by first responders to quickly identify and manage hazards. It is maintained by the [[Department of Transportation|DOT]] and is carried in most emergency service vehicles. The ERG provides guidance on initial isolation, protective actions, and emergency response for chemical, biological, radiological, and other hazardous materials.

General hazard response: 
- Do not enter the scene
- Remain upwind and uphill of scene
- Keep distance 
- Notify dispatch and request additional resources/units
