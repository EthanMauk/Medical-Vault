**Presumptive Signs of Death** 
- Unresponsive to painful stimuli
- Lack of carotid pulse or heartbeat
- Absence of chest rise and fall
- No deep tendon or corneal reflexes
- No systolic blood pressure
- Profound cyanosis
- Lowered body temperature

**Definitive Signs of Death**
- Mortal damage such as decapitation
- Dependent Lividity, blood settling to the lowest point of the body
- Rigor mortis, stiffening of body muscles
- Algor mortis, cooling of body temp to room temp
- Putrefaction, decomposition of body tissue
