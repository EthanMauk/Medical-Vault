The **Planes of the Body** are used to describe a particular view of the patients body, usually in respect to imaging. The patient is referenced in [[Anatomical Position]].These three imaginary dividing planes are
- **Coronal (frontal) plane** running vertically through the body dividing front and back
- **Sagittal (lateral) plane** running vertically dividing left and right
- **Transverse (axial) plane** running horizontally dividing top and bottom
