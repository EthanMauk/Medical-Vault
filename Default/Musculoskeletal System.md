The **Musculoskeletal System** refers to the partnership between bone and muscle. [[Muscle]] is a form of tissue that facilitates movement.  

Muscles are divides into three types: skeletal, smooth, and cardiac muscle. 

- [[Skeletal Muscle]] is a type of [[Striated Muscle|striated]] and [[Voluntary Muscle]].
- Cardiac muscle is [[Involuntary Muscle]] that is [[Striated Muscle|striated]] and generates its own electrical current
- Smooth muscle is all [[Involuntary Muscle]] not cardiac muscle

Skeletal muscles often function in [[Antagonistic Pairs]]. 

There are over 600 muscles in the musculoskeletal system.
