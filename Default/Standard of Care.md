**Standard of Care** is the level and manner of care that a reasonably prudent medical professional with similar training would provide under the same or similar circumstances.

It defines **how** you must act and behave while providing patient care.

Standard of care is determined by:
- Your **level of training and certification**
- Accepted **medical practices and guidelines**
- **State regulations**
- **Local protocols**
- What a similarly trained provider would reasonably do in the same situation

Failure to meet the standard of care may constitute **[[Negligence]]**, even if the intervention performed was technically within your scope of practice.
