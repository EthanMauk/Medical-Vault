# Albuterol  
**Albuterol** is a common [[bronchodilator]] that is used to treat airway constriction 

>[!indicators|cols]+ Indications #card
>> - Acute [[Asthma]]
>> - [[Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease|COPD]]
>> - [[Bronchitis]] 
>
>> - [[Pulmonary Edema]]
>> - Drug Overdose
>> - Suspected [[Hyperkalemia]]

>[!warning]+ Contraindications #card
>- Patient does **not have a prescription** for the inhaler 

## Administration
Albuterol is administered by inhalation, in 1 spray doses. 

>[!protocol]+ LA Country Restrictions #card
>**Los Angeles County** does not allow **EMTs** to carry [[bronchodilators]], but may assist patients with taking their own prescription 

## Mechanism #card
Albuterol works by stimulating 𝛽2-receptors on airway smooth muscle, activating adenyl cyclase to increase intracellular cAMP, which inhibits myosin phosphorylation and reduces intracellular calcium, resulting in bronchodilation

## Considerations
- Not recommended for patients under 12 years old

## Adverse Effects

>[!transparent|cols] Adverse Effects #card

>
