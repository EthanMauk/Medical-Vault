**Negligence** is the failure to meet the standard of care, resulting in harm to a patient.

To prove negligence, four elements must be established:
1. **Duty to Act** – You had a legal obligation to provide care.
2. **Breach of Duty** – You failed to meet the standard of care.
3. **Causation** – Your breach directly caused harm.
4. **Damages** – The patient suffered actual injury or loss.

All four elements must be present.
