**Critical Incident Stress Management (CISM)** is the system developed to address [[Acute Stress Reactions|acute stress]] situations and decrease the likelihood of [[Posttraumatic Stress Disorder|PTSD]] following them. 

Techniques
- Defusing: Short, small group interventions immediately after the event
- Debriefing: A structured smaller group session held within a week after crisis and reduce stress
- Peer Support: Specially trained colleagues who assist with stress reactions
