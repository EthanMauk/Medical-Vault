**Handover** is the transfer of important patient information and responsibility for patient care. Often involves transfer of [[Patient Care Report]] and ensures [[Continuity of Care]]. It is the most error prone part of care, and leads to the majority of litigation against EMS providers. Any missing or misleading information can cascade to disaster as it is repeated and distorted from EMT to Paramedic to LVN to RN to Physician.

When giving the Handover Report:
- Manage the environment, reduce [[Noise]], interruptions or distractions. 
- Ensure the [[ABCs]]
- Provide a structured report and documentation

A common report outline is shortened [[SBAR]]
- Situation: Name, patients name, [[Chief Complaint]]
- Background: Pertinent patient history
- Assessment: [[Vitals]], evaluation and observations
- Recommendation
