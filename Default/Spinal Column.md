The **Spinal Column** consists of 33 [[Vertebrae]], and acts to protect the spinal cord and support the body.
### Section of Spinal Column 
- Cervical (7)
	- Attaches to the skull 
- Thoracic (12)
	- One pair of ribs is attached to each vertebrae
- Lumbar (5)
- Sacral (5)
	- are fused together to form a single bone called the sacrum
- Coccygeal (5)
	- four vestigial vertebrae, which are fused together forming the coccyx or tailbone
