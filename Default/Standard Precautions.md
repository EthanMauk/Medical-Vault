**Standard Precautions** are a set of CDC developed guidelines for healthcare workers to use when treating patients, designed to prevent contracting infectious diseases.

- Assume every person is potentially infected and can spread infectious disease. 
- Use gloves whenever exposed to body fluids, switch gloves between patient contacts and wash hands between calls, using hand sanitizer whenever water is unavailable.
- Place sharps in sharps container directly after use, without setting it down
- Use pocket mask when administering breaths during [[CPR]]
